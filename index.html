Services
What I build for
manufacturing companies

Every service targets one goal — eliminate the repetitive engineering work that slows your team down.

01
SolidWorks Macros & Add-ins
Custom macros and enterprise-grade add-ins with UI, built on VB.NET and C#. From simple batch scripts to full-featured toolbar add-ins.
02
Drawing Automation
Auto-generate fabrication drawings from 3D models — views, annotations, title blocks, BOMs. Works for parts, assemblies, and full product families.
03
Parametric Configurators
Rule-driven 3D configurators: input parameters → complete models, assemblies, and drawings auto-generated in minutes. Built for configure-to-order products.
04
DXF / PDF Batch Export
Batch-export DXF files for laser/plasma cutting and PDFs for documentation. Hundreds of parts processed in minutes with correct file naming and folder structure.
05
BOM & GD&T Automation
Accurate BOMs auto-generated from assemblies, exported to Excel or ERP. GD&T and tolerance data extraction with balloon automation for revision control.
06
Standalone Engineering Apps
Custom desktop applications in VB.NET for estimating, quoting, data management, and file processing — built to integrate with your existing workflow.
